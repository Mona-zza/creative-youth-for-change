OUR PROJECTS

Education as a Right

Launched in November 2023, Education as a Right is our founding project. It brings learning back to those who were robbed of it especially Afghan girls and women banned from classrooms. Through online platforms, we provide:

  • Language programs (English, French, Chinese, German)
  • STEM courses and research mentorship
  • Leadership and debate trainings
  • Webinars, conferences, and learning circles

“Together, let’s educate all genders.”

Humanity Matters

A two-week online debate series gathering youth activists, gender advocates, and peacebuilders under the theme Humanity, Peacebuilding, Equality, Freedom, Democracy. We create space for voices from Afghanistan, Palestine, and Ukraine to confront shared struggles and write collective statements. Outcomes include:

  • A youth declaration on crises and solutions
  • Global solidarity letters
  • Public-facing advocacy content for policymakers

“When humanity matters, borders fall.”

She Leads (Upcoming, Oct 2025)

An advocacy campaign spotlighting Afghan girls’ leadership. Through stories, poetry, and art, we showcase the courage of young women leading in silence and in defiance. She Leads challenges global decision-makers to move from sympathy to structural change.

“She leads. The world follows.”
